Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled by the Receipt Scanner mobile application (“the App”).

1. Information Collection

The App does not collect or store any personal information on our servers.

Authentication is handled securely through third-party providers including Apple, Google, and Facebook. We do not access your personal profile data beyond the minimal credentials required to verify your identity.

2. Receipt Data

All scanned receipts are stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud backup, your data may be synchronized with your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to your receipt contents, thumbnails, or export data.

3. Optional Contact

In rare cases, we may need to contact you in relation to your use of the app, especially to assist with technical issues, to comply with legal requirements, or when required by authorized enforcement or legal bodies.

4. Legal Compliance

We reserve the right to share information with legal authorities if required by law, court order, or government regulation — within the scope permitted by applicable legislation.

5. Analytics & Advertising

The app does not currently use any analytics or advertising SDKs.

6. Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be made available through this page.
